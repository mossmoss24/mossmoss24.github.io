  • About

Emanuel Moss, PhD.

Emanuel Moss conducts ethnographic research on technology, focusing on integrated circuit design and issues of ethics, fairness, and accountability in AI systems. He is a Research Scientist at Intel Labs. His doctoral work produced an ethnography of machine learning investigating the role of data scientists as producers of knowledge.
